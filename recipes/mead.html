Mead

Description

Mead, also known as honey wine, is made by fermenting honey with water. It is a beverage favored by Odin himself. For most legends and myths, it is said that Odin only drinks mead and disregards every other drink and food.

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix the honey with the water
  2. Ferment concoction
